Adriana Molello

It was during a high school trip to New York City that I knew I wanted to live in the big apple. Originally from Denver Colorado, I moved to the city at the humble age of 18. In 2010 I received a BM in violin performance from Queens College and began pursuing my career as a violinist. From playing at Madison Square Garden, to the Met Ball uptown, it's been quite a ride. New York City has given me so many musical opportunities as well a chance to pursue a myriad of other interests, here are a few of them-

  • I'm partial to the Middle East & have traveled to Turkey, Lebanon, Israel & Palestine
  • I love to entertain!
  • I climbed the Great Wall of China
  • I've been busy teaching violin class out in East Brooklyn
  • I like learning about other artists, this week these words struck me
